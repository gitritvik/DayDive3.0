Happy Engineering Day

National Engineering Day

22 Happy Dhanteras

Published on September 15, 2023

Engineering Day

The day is celebrated to commemorate the birthday of Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya, a diwan of the former Mysore kingdom who is credited with pioneering engineering achievements

Paragraph 2: National Engineering Day is an important observance dedicated to celebrating the achievements and contributions of engineers in various fields. It serves as a reminder of the critical role engineers play in designing, building, and maintaining the infrastructure, technology, and systems that underpin modern society.

Paragraph 3: Engineers are responsible for innovative solutions that address complex challenges, from improving transportation and energy systems to advancing healthcare and communication technologies.

This day also aims to inspire the younger generation to consider careers in engineering, as the field holds immense potential for creating a sustainable and technologically advanced future
