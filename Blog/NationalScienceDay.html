History of National Science Day

Indian National Science Day is celebrated on February 28 every year to commemorate the discovery of the Raman Effect by Indian physicist Sir C.V. Raman in 1928. This groundbreaking discovery earned him the Nobel Prize in Physics, making him the first Asian to achieve this honor in the field of science. The day is a tribute to his invaluable contribution to science and an opportunity to recognize the significance of scientific research in India..
